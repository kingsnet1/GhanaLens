Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about GhanaLens

How can I submit a story or article to GhanaLens?

We welcome submissions from writers and journalists. Please send your story pitch or completed article to submissions@ghanalens.com with a brief bio and writing samples. Our editorial team will review your submission and get back to you within 7-10 business days.

How do I report an error in an article?

We strive for accuracy in all our reporting. If you've spotted an error, please email corrections@ghanalens.com with the article title, URL, and details about the correction needed. We appreciate your help in maintaining our editorial standards.

Can I reuse or republish GhanaLens content?

Our content is protected by copyright, but we do grant permission for limited reuse in certain circumstances. For republishing requests, please contact permissions@ghanalens.com with details about how you plan to use the content. We typically respond within 3-5 business days.

How can I advertise on GhanaLens?

We offer various advertising opportunities for businesses looking to reach our audience. Please contact our advertising team at ads@ghanalens.com to discuss available options, rates, and targeting capabilities. We'll work with you to create a campaign that meets your marketing objectives.

How do I subscribe to your newsletter?

You can subscribe to our newsletter by entering your email address in the newsletter form in our website footer. We send weekly updates with our top stories, exclusive content, and special features. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in each newsletter.
